# Product Thesis

---

## Problem

Dog owners already walk their dogs daily — but the walk is **forgettable**. Fitness apps count steps; social apps perform; calendars schedule. None treat the walk as **the dog's story unfolding in real life**.

---

## Insight

Owners do not lack motivation to walk. They lack **meaning, memory, and a gentle ritual** that says: *this hour mattered to them.*

PawStreak is a **memory engine disguised as a walk companion**.

---

## Solution (Fresh)

A cinematic mobile ritual:

1. **Today** surfaces how the dog feels and what kind of adventure fits.
2. **Plan** turns that into a concrete outing (local, no API required at launch).
3. **Active walk** is focused — timer, milestone copy, optional memory note.
4. **Reward** celebrates without gamification cringe.
5. **Journey** archives moments as a timeline, not a spreadsheet.

---

## Why now

- PWA + local mission engine = fast iteration without app store gate.
- Stitch-designed UI = premium emotional positioning from day one.
- Optional Supabase = save progress when ready, demo-first when not.

---

| Signal | Meaning |

- Community feed as core loop
- Real-time weather/location platform
