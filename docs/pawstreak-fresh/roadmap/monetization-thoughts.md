# Monetization Thoughts

> **Not launch scope.** Hypotheses for investor/product conversation only.

---

## Principles

1. Never paywall the **daily walk memory** — core ritual stays free
2. Charge for **depth, identity, and preservation** — not anxiety
3. No ads in active walk or Journey timeline

---

## Hypothesis A — PawStreak Plus

| Free | Plus |
|------|------|
| Daily missions + Journey | Photo memories cloud backup |
| Local storage | Multi-device sync priority |
| Basic Path | Advanced season recaps / export |

Price anchor: **$4–7/mo** — below streaming, above tip jar.

---

## Hypothesis B — Seasonal packs

Cosmetic / narrative **region packs** tied to deferred Packs system — one-time purchase, not loot boxes.

---

## Hypothesis C — Physical

Printed memory books from Journey — partnership or print-on-demand.

> High margin, low frequency — brand marketing value.

---

## What we will not do (likely)

- Sell owner location data
- Engagement-maximizing notification spam
- Pay-to-win streaks

---

## Metrics before monetization

- D7 / D30 retention on completed walks
- Memory capture rate
- Account save conversion
