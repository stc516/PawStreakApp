# Brand Voice

---

## Personality

| We are | We are not |
|--------|------------|
| Warm | Corporate |
| Specific | Vague |
| Gentle | Pushy |
| Playful (dog-level) | Snarky (human-level) |
| Cinematic | Cartoony chaos |

---

## Sample lines

**Onboarding**  
"Every day is part of their story."

**Today**  
"Bailey's up for something social."

**Completion**  
"Bailey had a great day."

**Save progress**  
"Save Bailey's story — pick up on any phone."

---

## Grammar

- Second person sparingly — **dog is subject**
- Contractions OK (`they're`, `let's`)
- No ALL CAPS except small eyebrow labels
- Exclamation marks: **rare** (reward only)

---

## Words we use

adventure, memory, journey, path, story, walk, spot, golden hour

## Words we avoid

quest log, grind, leaderboard, user, consumer, utilize, synergy
